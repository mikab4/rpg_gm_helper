# Session 001

## Summary

The party arrived in Ashfall after sunset and learned that Captain Mira Voss had sealed the north gate after a series of thefts. At the Broken Lantern tavern, they heard rumors that the Ember Knives were looking for an old brass key connected to the Sunken Archive.

Later that night, the group followed a smuggler toward the river district and found signs that Brother Calen had already been searching the warehouse cellars. The trail suggested that the brass key may open a hidden chamber beneath the archive.
